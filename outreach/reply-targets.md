## The method (read this first)

Cold "will you use my product" = low reply rate. Three higher-reply motions, ranked by ease:

**1. Reply-in-public (highest reply, zero gatekeeper).**
Find the exact public post where the person already named your problem, reply with "built the thing you're describing," link, ask for their take. Not a pitch — a contribution to their thread. Warm targets that already exist:
- **11x** publicly had a duplicate-send incident (TechCrunch). Reply/DM Prabhav: "saw the Alice duplicate-send story — built exactly-once for agent actions so a retry can't double-fire. Want it free?"
- **Simon Willison** writes the "lethal trifecta" / MCP-injection posts. Reply with the approval-gate design, ask his take. He blogs tools he finds technically interesting = the real prize.
- **Nuno Campos** co-wrote the "MCP fad or fixture" debate citing tool-call failure rates. That's your feature. Reply with how you handle it.
- The **e-commerce operator's post** you pasted (shadow→assisted→autonomous, hand-built approval gates) — reply "built the graduation ladder as a product, want early access?"

**2. Founder-to-founder DM for design partners (medium reply).**
Ask for their war story, not their money. "15 min — what breaks when your agent takes a real action?" Founders answer founders. Offer Revive free during pilot. Money-movement + high-volume-send segments feel this pain continuously (not episodically) → they reply.

**3. VC portfolio-intro ask (easy reply, wrong framing avoided).**
Do NOT pitch VCs to *use* it. Ask: "which of your portfolio companies run agents in prod? I built the safety layer, happy to give it to them free." Makes the VC look useful to their founders → high reply. One intro fans out to a dozen portfolio cos.

**Concrete week:** 4 reply-in-public today (11x, Willison, Campos, operator post) → 8 design-partner DMs (money/send segment below) → 5 VC portfolio-intro asks → list on Glama + PR to awesome-mcp lists (free permanent funnel). Track replies, not sends.
